Baofeng UV-5R

I like my new handhelds. Of course, you get what you pay for, but €20 for 2 handhelds is a real bargain.

As with any handheld the stock antenna is crap and to receive anything you need an outside antenna, but you can't beat this price.

My first priority is to get RG213 coax cable, some PL259 and some SO239 connectors and to get a antenna outside. Jan PA0VJB, a local radio amateur has promised me a spare J-antenna.
